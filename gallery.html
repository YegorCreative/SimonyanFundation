OUR TEAM IN ARTSAKH

Our team was in Artsakh on September 27th. Early in the morning we woke up from the sounds of bombs. At first, we thought that it would stop shortly, but the shelling continued. They were hitting randomly in the direction of Stepanakert and Shushi.
