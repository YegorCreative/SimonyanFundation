WAR BRINGS
DEVASTATION

We see many people in extreme need around us, some of them have lost their homes, lost their husbands, their children, brothers. The mourning in Armenia cannot be explained with words.

Those days more then 250 families of victims have received gift boxes.

During those difficult days, donations from people allowed us to support more than 600 families living in Armenia and Artsakh…. (We supported families in danger, those who lost parents, families with many children, families of those killed or missing in action as well as many others..)

We were able to provide their basic needs, clothing, food etc. We are so thankful for your support. Still there are hundreds of families that need help. You can be a part of this good work.
